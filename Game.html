This all day every day game consists of two or more people getting together and speaking on what, if anything, is distracting us from this present moment. Thoughts of the past and the future, however valid, can sometime detract from our experience of this NOW moment. This clearing process opens the space to speak these thoughts and be heard, so that we are able to let them go and be in full enjoyment of the moment. While also being mindful of what is important to us as well. Through a series of three questions we, Camp Gratitude, intend to guide Burners back to enjoying the precious time we have here at Burning Man. The first question is usually along the lines of "What are you present to" followed by "While you are present to that how do you feel?" With the listener recreating what they heard back to the speaker, so there is a clear understanding and reflection for the person being cleared. After that there are two "Questions of the Day." The first QotD is directed towards an aspect of our ego or shadow side, and bringing it into light. By being conscious of these processes, and how we feel while present to them, we can work through them and utilize them.

Burning Camp Mazu Workshop

Mazu

Our theme camp work shop includes bua-buei ,message ,talisman and fortune stick. Bua buei" Bua-buei means throwing divine blocks. Buei blocks are very common divination tools in Taiwanese temples,which are used in pairs and thrown to get a yes or no answer from gods. They are made out of wood and carved into a crescent shape.Each block is round on one side (known as the Yin side) and flat on the other (known as the Yang side). When throwing the blocks, there are three possible results: One block flat and another block round is a ‘yes’ answer.Both blocks flat is a “maybe” answer. Both blocks round is a ‘no’ answer. ​ "Fortune Sticks" During a ritual, the querent will light an incense, Bua-buei and accompanied by drawing fortune sticks to clarify an oracle. ​ "Talisman" We will use talismanic stamp or giving an amulet to participant for good health and good luck.
